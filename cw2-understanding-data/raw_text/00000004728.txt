And, con­suls, I beseech you, that if this business is put intoyour hands by the senate, you will make me andMarcus Foslius the first objects of your examinations ;it shall be manifested that we owe our safety from suchimputations to our own innocence, not to the dignityof office.' He then abdicated the dictatorship, as didMarcus Foslius immediately after his office of masterof the horse ; and being the first brought to trial be­fore the consuls, for to them the senate had com­mitted the business, they were most honorably ac­quitted of all the charges brought by the nobles. EvenPublilius Philo, who had so often been invested withthe highest honors, and had performed so many emi­nent services, both at home and abroad, being disa­greeable to the nobility, was brought to trial, and ac­quitted.
