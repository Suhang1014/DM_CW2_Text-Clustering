While a gold mine was thus opened for the mercantileclass, and the members of the new partnership constituted agreat financial power imposing even for the government—a"senate of merchants "—a definite sphere of public actionwas at the same time assigned to them in the jurycourts. The field of the criminal procedure which by rightfell to be brought before the burgesses was among theRomans from the first very narrow, and was, as we alreadyobserved (P.l 12), still further narrowed by Gracchus.
