In the seasons ofpeace and affluence, communities as well as individuals havetheir tempers under better regulation, because not liable tothat violence which flows from necessity. But war, whichsnatcheth from them their daily subsistence, is the teacher ofviolence, and assimilates the passions of men to their presentcondition. By these means were cities harassed with seditions.
