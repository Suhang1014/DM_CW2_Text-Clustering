In the mean­while his general Adarman advanced from Babylon, tra­versed the desert, passed the Euphrates, insulted the su­burbs of Antioch, reduced to ashes the city of Apamea,and laid the spoils of Syria at the feet of his master, whoseperseverance, in the midst of winter, at length subvertedthe bulwark of the east. But these losses, which asto­nished the provinces and the court, produced a salutaryeffect in the repentance and abdication of the emperorJustin; a new spirit arose in the Byzantine councils;and a truce of three years was obtained by the prudenceof Tiberius.
