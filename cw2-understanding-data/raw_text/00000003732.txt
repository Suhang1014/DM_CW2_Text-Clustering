And thus did Josephus dispose of both his bodies ofmen : but then for the useless part of the citizens, the womenand children, when they saw their city encompassed by athreefold army (for none of the usual guards that had beenfighting before were removed;) when they also saw not onlythe walls thrown down, but their enemies, with swords intheir hands, as also the hilly country above them shining withtheir weapons, and the darts in the hands of the Arabianarchers, they made a final and lamentable outcry of destruc­tion, as if the misery were not only threatened, but actuallycome upon them already.
