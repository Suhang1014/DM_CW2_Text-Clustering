The augurs assisted atthe ceremony ; the portion of the bride wasopenly paid (e); the genial bed was dis­played to view; nuptial torches were light­ed up; the whole was public, not even ex­cepting the endearments which, in a naturalmarriage, decency reserves for the shadesof night.
