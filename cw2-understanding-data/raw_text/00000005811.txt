The island Siphnos had gold-mines ,and they were ordered by Apollo to fend a tenth of theproduce of these mines to Delphos; in consequence ofwhich they built a treasury, and sent with it a tenth ofthe produce of their mines. Afterwards, however, throughtheir immoderate desire of accumulating wealth, they ne­glected to send the tenth of their riches to Delphos; andin consequence of this their gold mines were destroyedby an inundation of the sea.
