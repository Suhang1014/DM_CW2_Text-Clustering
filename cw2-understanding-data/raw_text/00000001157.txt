The Grecian camp had stretchedtwelve miles along the shore from the Sigaean to theRhaetean promontory; and the flanks of the armywere guarded by the bravest chiefs who fought underthe banners of Agamemnon. The first of thosepromontories was occupied by Achilles with his in­vincible Myrmidons, and the dauntless Ajax pitchedhis tents on the other.
