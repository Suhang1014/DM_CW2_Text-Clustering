And now did Titus consult with his commanders whatwas to be done. Those that were of the warmest tempersthought he should bring the whole army against the city, andstorm the wall; for that hitherto no more than a part of theirarmy had fought with the Jews ; but that in case the entiroarmy was to come at once, they would not be able to sustaintheir attacks, but would be overwhelmed by their darts.
