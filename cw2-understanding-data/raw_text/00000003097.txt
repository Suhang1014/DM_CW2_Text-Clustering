At which time Herod released to his subjects the third partof their taxes, under pretence indeed of relieving them, after thedearth they had had; but the main reason was, to recover theirgood-will, which he now wanted, for they were uneasy at him,because of the innovations he had introduced in their practices, ofthe dissolution of their religion, and of the disuse of their own cus­toms;
