The careless glance which men of wit andlearning condescended to cast on the Christian revelation, served onlyto confirm their hasty opinion, and to persuade them, that the prin­ciple which they might have revered, of the divine unity, was de­faced by the wild enthusiasm, and annihilated by the airy specula­tions of the new sectaries.
