The most skilfulcharioteers obtained the first place in his confidenceand esteem; their merit was profusely rewarded : theemperor feasted in their houses, and presented theirchildren at the baptismal font; and, while he ap­plauded his own popularity, he affected to blame thecold and stately reserve of his predecessors.
