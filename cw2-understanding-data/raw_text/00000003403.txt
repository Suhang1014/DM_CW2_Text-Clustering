The chiefs of Armenia andScythia, who fled from hostile or religious perse­cution, were hospitably entertained: their followerswere encouraged to build new cities and to cultivatewaste lands; and many spots, both in Europe andAsia, preserved the name, the manners, or at least,the memory, of these national colonies.
