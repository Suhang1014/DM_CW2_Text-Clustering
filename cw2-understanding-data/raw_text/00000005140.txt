The proconsul transmitted him tothe Prætorian præfect of Italy; and Fælix, who disdained even togive an evasive answer, was at length beheaded at Venufia, inLucania, a place on which the birth of Horace has conferredfame This precedent, and perhaps some imperial rescript, whichwas issued in consequence of it, appeared to authorize the gover­nors of provinces, in punishing with death the refusal of the Chri­stians to deliver up their sacred books. There were undoubtedlymany persons who embraced this opportunity of obtaining the crownof martyrdom; but there were likewise too many who purchasedan ignominious life, by discovering and betraying the holy scriptureinto the hands of infidels.
