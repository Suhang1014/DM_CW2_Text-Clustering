Large stones, too,and fragments of rocks torn from Parnassus, fell on theBarbarians, as the destined mark at which they aimed.Nor was it only one or two that died by this means; butthirty, or still more, as they stood upon 'guard or slept inthe fame place, were at once dashed in pieces by the fall­ing rocks.
