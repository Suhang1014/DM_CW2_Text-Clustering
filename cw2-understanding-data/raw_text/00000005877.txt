When the person that descended toTrophonius returns, the sacrisicers immediately place himon a throne, which they call the throne of Mnemosyne,and which stands not far from the adytum. Then they askhim what he has either seen or heard, and afterwards de­liver him to certain persons appointed for this purpose,who bring him to the temple of Good Fortune, and theGood Dæmon, while he is yet full of terror, and with­out any knowledge either of himself, or of those that arenear him.
