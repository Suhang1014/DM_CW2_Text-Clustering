Butthe wise monarch, instead of aspiring to the conquestof Germany, confined his attention to the importantand laborious defence of the Gallic frontier, againstan enemy, whose strength was renewed by a streamof daring volunteers, which incessantly flowed fromthe most distant tribes of the North.
