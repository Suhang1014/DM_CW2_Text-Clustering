During the time of these transactions in Macedo­nia the other consul, Lucius Lentulus, who had stayedat Rome, held an assembly for the election of censors.Out of many illustrious men who stood candidates,were chosen Publius Cornelius Scipio Africanus andPublius jElius Pastus.
