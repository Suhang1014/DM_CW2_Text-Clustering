He proceeded by rapid marches toVesontium (z), a city in league with Vin­dex. The inhabitants refused to opentheir gates. Virginius pitched his camp,determined to lay siege to the place. Vin­dex advanced to the relief of his confede­rates. The two armies were in sight ofeach other.
