Now such men as he are more zealous to justify asacrilegious king than to write what is just and what is trueabout us, and about our temple : for when they are desirousof gratifying Antiochus, and of concealing that perfidiousnessand sacrilege which he was guilty of with regard to our na­tion when he wanted money, they endeavor to disgrace us,and tell lies even relating to futurities.
