A son, or, by the lawsof Justinian, even a daughter, could no longer bedisinherited by their silence: they were compelledto name the criminal, and to specify the offence; andthe justice of the emperor enumerated the sole causesthat could justify such a violation of the first prin­ciples of nature and society.
