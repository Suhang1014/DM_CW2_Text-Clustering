When the ambassadors, who had been sentto offer restitution, returned, without concluding apeace, he said in an assembly ' that you may not thinkthat no purpose has been effected by this embassy, beassured that whatever degree of anger the deities ofheaven had conceived against us, on account of the in­fraction of the treaty, has been hereby expiated. What more, Roman,do I owe to thee? what to the treaty? what to the gods,the guarantees of the treaty ?
