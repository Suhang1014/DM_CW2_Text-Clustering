Scipio, ordering the boy to be takencare of, finished what business was to be done at thetribunal; then, retiring into his pavilion, he called theyouth, and asked him whether he wished to return toMasinissa? To which the other, his eyes suffusedwith tears of joy, replied, that above all things it waswhat he wished.
