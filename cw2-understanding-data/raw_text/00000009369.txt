Meanwhile, both hope and anxiety daily in­creased in equal proportion; nor could people judgewith certainty whether it was a proper subject of re­joicing that Hannibal had, at the end of sixteen years,departed from Italy, and thereby left the possession ofit open to the Roman people, or whether they had notrather cause of fear in his having carried his army safeinto Africa.
